Adjacency: Numbers must be next to each other (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) or at the end of one line and the beginning of the next.
Elimination: Tap the pair to remove them from the board.
Empty Spaces: Empty cells between numbers do not block a match; only other numbers do.
Adding Lines: If you run out of moves, you can add the remaining numbers to the bottom, adding more pairs to the grid.
Winning: Clear all numbers from the grid.¬†

Strategies & Tips
Clear Small Numbers: Focus on merging smaller numbers first to keep the board open.
Check Button: Use the "Check" button to move remaining numbers together, creating new potential pairs.
Undo: You can undo the previous elimination if you make a mistake, but it is limited.
Hints: Use hints if you get stuck.
Rows first: Removing full rows earns bonus points (+10 points).¬†

Scoring
