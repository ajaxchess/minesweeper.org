Highlight errors toggle (very popular with learners)
Night mode (already table stakes for puzzle sites)
Hide timer / non-competitive timer (makes the game feel lower stakes for casual players)
Gray out completed numbers (quality of life for serious players)
Show board coordinates (A1–G7 labels)
Auto-submit when puzzle is logically complete

6. Authenticated user features (your biggest structural advantage)
Since you have Google Sign-In:

Personal solve history and streaks
Personal best time tracking per difficulty
Win/loss stats and calendar heatmap (like GitHub contributions)
Puzzle completion badges ("7-day streak", "First solve under 60s")

These create retention loops puzzle-minesweeper.com literally cannot replicate without a complete rebuild.
7. Mobile-first polish

A highly requested feature in the minesweeper community is guaranteed-solvable-by-logic puzzles. If your generator can guarantee this for Tentaizu (which is much more tractable on a 7×7 than classic Minesweeper), prominently advertise it — puzzle-minesweeper.com does not mention this at all.
