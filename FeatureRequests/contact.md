# Contact Us

Have a question, found a bug, or want to share feedback about Minesweeper.org? We'd love to hear from you.

## Get in Touch

**Email:** [rcross@fiberxl.com](mailto:rcross@fiberxl.com)

We typically respond within a few business days. When reporting a bug, please include the browser and device you were using, the game mode you were playing, and a description of what happened — this helps us track down the issue faster.

## What to Contact Us About

- **Bug reports** — Something not working as expected? Let us know.
- **Feature requests** — Have an idea for a new game mode, variant, or improvement? We're always open to suggestions.
- **Leaderboard issues** — If you believe a score was recorded incorrectly or suspect foul play, reach out and we'll investigate.
- **Privacy and data requests** — To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) or email us directly.
- **General feedback** — We appreciate hearing what players enjoy and what could be better.
