Replay button after you finish the game.

This feature is for Minesweeper Classic, Daily and No-Guess. 

The game should record a timestamped event log of every click (left, right, chord). The log should be recorded to sessionStorage. If the game ends in a win, the log should also be stored to the server-side DB.

After you finish a minesweeper game (either win or lose), you can click a Play button.

The game starts to replay from the beginning of the game.
The Play button becomes a Pause button so you can stop the action and view what you were doing. The granularity should be time-based.

There is a play bar, so you can select any point in your replay sequence.  Works like a streaming where you can move the play bar to a point in the game. See Rewind.png.

It would be nice to have a 2x, 4x, 8x, 16x for your replay.

Rewind and Replay are entirely separate features.
