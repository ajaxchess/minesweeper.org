League of Minesweeper

We want to reimplement League of Minesweeper in an iOS and Android app.  The games will use the minesweeper.org high scores list and have ads:

https://minesweeper.fandom.com/wiki/League_of_Minesweeper
League of Minesweeper, also known as liánméng (联盟), or LM, is chinese based minesweeper app. Despite the name, League of Minesweeper consists of 6 different puzzle games which are minesweeper, schulte grid, 15puzzle, 2048, sudoku, and nonosweeper. 
(League of Minesweeper was available for download on Android at TapTap.)

Minesweeper
Game rules
Turn on all safe blocks according to the numbers on the blocks. The numbers indicate the number of mines around the eight blocks.

Mode
Minesweeper comes in 5 different modes which are

Daily: a daily puzzle where every players get the same board.
Custom: can generate board up 50% in mine density and 500x500 with 125000 mines in size
Classic: Beginner, Intermediate, and Expert
Endless: Endless
Nonguessing: puzzle without penalty including community submissions
Schulte Grid
Game rules
Tap on the numbers in ascending order.

Mode
Schulte Grid comes in 6 different modes which are

Easy: A number tile disappear after being tapped
Blind(Easy): A number tile and numbers disappear after being tapped
Normal: Normal
Blind(Normal): Numbers disappear after being tapped
Easy mix: A number tile disappear and numbers change position after being tapped
Mix: Numbers change position after being tapped
Each mode have different board sizes that players can choose (3x3, 4x4, 5x5, 6x6, 7x7, 8x8, 9x9, and 10x10)

15Puzzle
Game rules
Move the squares in the square area, arrange all the squares from left to right, top to bottom, and small to large, click on the digital square to move or drag the blamk square to slide (the slide which needs to be turned on).

Mode
Custom: Generate your own board within 3x3-10x10 sizes
Normal: Normal
Blind: Numbers disappear after being tapped
Link: Play x amount of the same board
Relay: Start from 3x3 upto the chosen board size
Each mode have different board sizes that players can choose (3x3, 4x4, 5x5, 6x6, 7x7, 8x8, 9x9, and 10x10)

2048
Game rules
Move in one direction to merge thee squares of the same number into a larger number. Each movement will generate a 2 or 4 at a random position until all the squares cannot be moved and the game is over.

Mode
Upon reaching the target score, player can choose to end the game or continue playing to get higher score.

3x3: Target score 256
4x4: Target score 2048
5x5: Target score 16384
Sudoku
Game rules
Fill in the numbers in the blanks so that each row, column, and each nine-square grid contains numbers 1-9

Mode
Easy: worth 1 point upon completion
Normal: worth 2 points upon completion
Hard: worth 3 points upon completion
Hell: worth 5 points upon completion
Nonosweeper
Game rules
Open all safe squares based on numbers, or just mark all mine squares. The numbers in each row and column indicate the number of adjacent mines in the row and column.

Mode
Beginner: 6x6 grid with 27 mines
Intermediate: 10x10 grid with 64 mines
Expert: 15x12 grid with 90 mines
Genius: 20x15 grid with 148 mines
