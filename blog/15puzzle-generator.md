# Upload Your Own Photo to the 15-Puzzle Generator

We just launched a new feature: the **[15-Puzzle Generator](https://minesweeper.org/other/15puzzle/generator)**. Upload any photo, scramble it into a sliding tile puzzle, and share the link with anyone. No login required to play — just to save.

## How I tested it

I wanted a nice photo to use as a demo. Naturally, since this is *Lady Di's Mines*, I went looking for a royalty-free photo of Diana, Princess of Wales.

It turns out that's harder than it sounds. Image copyright is notoriously difficult to verify. Even photos on Wikipedia carry licensing restrictions — the photographer holds the copyright regardless of where the image appears. A useful tool for checking is [Google Image Search with the Creative Commons filter](https://www.google.com/search?q=diana+spencer&udm=2) (Tools → Creative Commons licenses), but even then, you have to read the fine print on each result.

After a good bit of searching, I gave up on finding a clean royalty-free Diana photo. And then it hit me: of course there are no royalty-free photos of Diana.

**She's a Princess. Nothing about her is royalty-free.**

## What you should upload instead

The best photos for this puzzle are personal ones — photos you took yourself. Family trips, pets, a favourite view from your window. You own the copyright on everything you shoot, so there's no ambiguity.

The puzzle works in two modes:

- **Tiles** — the photo is sliced across the 15 tiles and scrambled with them. Solving it reassembles your image.
- **Reveal on win** — tiles show numbers as usual, but solving the puzzle reveals your photo underneath as a reward.

## Moderation

Because this is user-generated content, we have an admin review page where we can remove anything problematic. Keep it friendly — puzzles are publicly accessible the moment you save them.

You can save up to 32 puzzles. Your saved list is also available from your [profile page](https://minesweeper.org/profile).

Give it a try at **[minesweeper.org/other/15puzzle/generator](https://minesweeper.org/other/15puzzle/generator)**.
