# Taking the Show on the Road

We've spent a lot of time making [minesweeper.org](https://minesweeper.org) the best place to play in a browser. Now we're bringing it with you.

---

## The Apps Are Here

We've built two native apps — one for Apple devices, available now, and one for Android, currently in testing and coming soon.

If you've been playing on the site, the experience carries over. Your leaderboard access goes with you. The same game you know, in your pocket.

---

## No Ads. No Waiting.

Here's the part that matters most: finish a game — win or lose — and you can start the next one immediately. No unskippable ads. No countdown. Just sweep again.

That's not something most free minesweeper apps can say.

---

## Where to Get It

The iOS app is live now on the App Store. Android is in testing and won't be far behind. We'll post the link here when it lands.

If you have an iPhone or iPad: **[get it here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/minesweeper-org/id6761314113)**.
