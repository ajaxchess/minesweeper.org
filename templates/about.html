About

This site is a tribute to Diana, Princess of Wales.

Why is Diana on this site?

Diana Spencer, Princess of Wales, devoted much of her life to banning landmines. Her trip to Angola in 1996 brought worldwide attention to the campaign to ban landmines. Her efforts helped the International Campaign to Ban Landmines win the Nobel peace prize in 1997.

Most countries have signed a treaty in Ottawa outlawing the use of landmines, but the United States, China and Russia have not.

I hope that this site increases awareness of the movement to ban landmines. Diana's wish that all landmines be banned in her lifetime will not come true, but you can make it come true in yours.

The HALO Trust

The HALO Trust is an organization that digs up landmines. You can contact them at halotrust.org.
