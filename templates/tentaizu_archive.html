About the Daily Puzzle Format

Each day's Tentaizu puzzle is generated from the date as a seed — the same date always produces the same puzzle. This means you can share a puzzle date with a friend and you'll both solve an identical grid, comparing times and approaches even if you played at different hours.

All generated puzzles are verified to have a unique solution reachable through pure logical deduction — no guessing required. If you encounter a puzzle that seems to require guessing, try revisiting your deductions; there will always be a logical path.

The daily puzzle resets at midnight UTC. Every puzzle has its own permanent leaderboard — scores submitted for a past puzzle remain visible on that puzzle's permalink page.
