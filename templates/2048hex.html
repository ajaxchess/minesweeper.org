How to Play

The board is a hexagonal grid of 19 circles — 18 playable tiles and one fixed stone in the center. Tiles slide in 6 directions until they hit a wall or the stone. Two tiles with the same value merge into one with double the value. Reach 2048 to win!

  • Desktop: Q / E / A / D / Z / X — or use the buttons below the board
  • Arrow keys: ← / → move left and right
  • Mobile: swipe in any of the 6 hex directions

The stone blocks tiles — they cannot pass through or merge with it. This splits each row and diagonal into two independent sections.
