Standard ⚡ Quick 🤖 vs Bot 🏆 Leaderboard 👑 Rankings

Play a real PvP-rules match against a computer opponent. Both you and the bot receive the same board and start simultaneously — whoever scores higher wins. The bot uses an iterative constraint solver: it reads every revealed number, propagates deductions across the board, and only guesses when no safe move can be proven. The difficulty setting controls how fast it clicks and how often it plays a random move instead of solving.

Tips for beating the bot

  • Start center, chord aggressively. A large opening cascade and fast chord-clicking produces more tile reveals per second than the bot's fixed click rate — even on Hard.
  • Easy is a real warmup. With a 40% random-move rate, Easy will hit mines and stall. Use it to get comfortable with the PvP board size before stepping up.
  • Hard is genuinely hard. At 5 clicks per second with a full solver, the Hard bot clears tiles quickly and consistently. To win you need sub-30s Beginner-level play and zero wasted clicks.
  • Scoring is the same as live PvP. 5 points per tile revealed, plus a time bonus of (300 − seconds) × percentRevealed. Clearing more of the board fast matters as much as not hitting mines.
