How to play Tentaizu

Tentaizu is a 7×7 logic puzzle with exactly 10 hidden mines.

  • Revealed numbers show how many mines are in the 8 surrounding cells.
  • Click any unknown cell to cycle: 💣 (mine) → ✓ (safe) → ? (unknown).
  • Use logic to deduce which cells contain mines.
  • The game never ends on a wrong guess — keep refining until all 10 mines are correctly flagged.
  • You win when exactly the 10 mine cells are flagged and no safe cells are flagged.
