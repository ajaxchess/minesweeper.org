What is Tametsi Hex?

Tametsi Hex combines the deduction-only logic of Tametsi with the hexagonal grid of Hexsweeper. Every cell has up to 6 neighbours, and every puzzle is designed to be solved with pure logic — no guessing needed.

The four puzzles here are tutorials. They introduce the rules on progressively larger boards and mechanics before the main campaign launches.

How to Play

  • Some cells start revealed (outlined in blue) showing their mine-neighbour count.
  • Left-click a hidden cell to reveal it. Hitting a mine ends the puzzle.
  • Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to flag a suspected mine.
  • A cell showing 0 has no adjacent mines — its neighbours are revealed automatically.
  • A cell showing ? is safe but gives no information — it could have any number of mine neighbours.
  • Reveal every non-mine cell to win.
