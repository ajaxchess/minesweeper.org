Tentaizu Easy – Daily 5×5 Logic Puzzle

About Easy Mode

Tentaizu Easy is a 5×5 grid with exactly 6 hidden mines — a gentler introduction to the Tentaizu format. All the same rules apply: numbered clues show how many mines are in the 8 surrounding cells, and your goal is to flag all 6 mines using pure logic. No guessing ever required.

The smaller board and fewer mines make deductions faster and the solution more immediate — ideal for a quick daily warm-up or for players new to logic puzzles.

How to Play

  • Revealed numbers show how many mines are in the 8 surrounding cells.
  • Left-click to cycle: ✓ safe → ⭐ mine → ? unknown.
  • Right-click to cycle: ⭐ mine → ✓ safe → ? unknown.
  • You win when all 6 mine cells are flagged and no safe cell is flagged.
