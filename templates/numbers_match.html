About Numbers Match

What is Numbers Match?

Numbers Match is a number-pairing puzzle where you clear a grid by connecting pairs of matching numbers. Two numbers match if they are identical (1 and 1, 7 and 7) or if they sum to 10 (1 and 9, 2 and 8, 3 and 7, 4 and 6, 5 and 5). To remove a pair, the two numbers must be connected by a clear line — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal — with only empty cells between them.

A unique twist is the row wrap: the last cell of a row is considered adjacent to the first cell of the next row, extending horizontal connections across the line boundary. This creates unexpected matching opportunities that reward careful scanning.

When no valid moves remain, tap Add Lines to append the remaining numbers to the bottom of the board, creating new adjacencies. The board grows until you can clear everything.

How to Score Points

  • Basic match (+1): Every pair you remove earns at least one point.
  • Far Apart bonus (+1–4): Matching numbers with empty cells between them earns a bonus — one point per empty cell, up to four. Seek out long-range matches for maximum points.
  • Row clear (+10): When an entire row becomes empty, you earn a 10-point bonus. Plan ahead to clear full rows.
  • Board clear (+150): Clear the entire board for a large bonus and a spot on the leaderboard.

Strategy Tips

  1. Scan for long-range pairs. Far Apart bonuses add up quickly. Before making an obvious adjacent match, look for the same pair connected through empty space.
  2. Target row clears. A row with only one or two numbers remaining is close to a +10 bonus. Prioritise clearing those numbers to trigger the bonus.
  3. Use hints sparingly. You only have 9 hints per game. Save them for when you are genuinely stuck, not just for confirmation.
  4. Undo strategically. With only 3 undos, save them for accidental taps or missed scoring opportunities, not routine exploration.
  5. Add Lines as a last resort. More rows mean more cells to scan. Use Add Lines only when no valid move exists anywhere on the current board.
