The app also contains supporting screens How-to-Play Minesweeper and Strategy & Tips

It features the 
Classic minesweeper games
  Beginner
  Intermediate
  Expert
  Leaderboard

The iOS app will only support English (at least this first version).

Generally each of these games consist of instructions, 
a board that you can interact with, 
a way to share the board with other people,
a timer, and 
a global high scores list.
